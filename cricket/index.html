Knowing the game

Cricket is an international game between two teams one of which would be batting and the other fielding,making it a play between the bat ,ball,fielders. The team batting first sets up a total for the opponent to chase ,if they defend the total successfully they end up winning the match else they lose it . Usually the sport played in the following three formats: 1)Test 2)ODI 3)T20
